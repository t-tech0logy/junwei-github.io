About Coach

Developing Confident, Disciplined & Skilled Badminton Players

Liao Junwei is a highly experienced and patient badminton coach with over 15 years of coaching experience, nurturing players from beginners to elite-level athletes. His coaching approach emphasises strong technical foundations, tactical awareness, and mental resilience.

Coach Liao holds internationally recognised certifications and has represented Singapore as a coach at the ASEAN Para Games 2023. Beyond performance, he instils values of sportsmanship, discipline, and teamwork to develop well-rounded athletes both on and off the court.
